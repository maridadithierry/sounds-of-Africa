Wizkid

Biography

Wizkid is known for his Afro-fusion music style, which blends dancehall, reggae, Afrobeat, pop, hip hop, and R&B which has played a pivotal role in popularizing African music on the global stage. Wizkid's international breakthrough came with his feature on Drake's chart-topping single "One Dance" in 2016, which became a global hit.
