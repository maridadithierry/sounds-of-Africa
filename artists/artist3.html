Biography

Kizz Daniel is known professionally as Kizz Daniel, is a prominent Nigerian singer and songwriter celebrated for his contributions to the Afrobeats genre. He first gained widespread recognition in 2014 with his hit single "Woju," which marked the beginning of his successful music career12.
