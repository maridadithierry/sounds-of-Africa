Burna Boy

Born on July 2, 1991 in Port Harcourt, Nigeria, Burna Boy started making music at age 10. He attended Montessori International primary school and Corona Secondary School in Lagos. He studied media technology at the University of Sussex and media communications and culture at Oxford Brookes University.

Burna Boy is known for known for his Afro-fusion music style, which blends dancehall, reggae, Afrobeat, pop, hip hop, and R&B
