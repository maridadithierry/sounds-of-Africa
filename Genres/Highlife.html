Highlife

Description

Highlife originated in Ghana in the early 20th century and spread across West Africa. It combines traditional Akan melodies with Western instruments like guitars and trumpets. Catchy guitar riffs and horn sections define the genre.

History and Key Characteristics

Highlife gained popularity in nightclubs and social gatherings. It influenced other genres, such as Afrobeat. The fusion of African rhythms and Western jazz created a unique sound that resonated with audiences worldwide.

Prominent Artists
