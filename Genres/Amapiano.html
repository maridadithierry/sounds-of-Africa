Amapiano

Description

Amapiano is a South African house music style that emerged in the 2010s. It features deep basslines, piano melodies, and slow tempos. Log drum patterns are a signature characteristic of this genre.

History and Key Characteristics

The genre grew from South African townships, combining elements of deep house and jazz. Amapiano is celebrated for its unique rhythms and soulful melodies, making it a dancefloor favorite.

Prominent Artists

  • Kabza De Small
  • DJ Maphorisa
  • Sha Sha
